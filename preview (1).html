About

アトリエについて

I,V

人形たちが暮らす街の片隅で、
今日も一着ずつ。

I,Vは、人形のための服を一着ずつ手で仕立てる小さなアトリエです。

生成りの布、淡い色、レース、リボン。古い街や静かな森の記憶をたどるように、型紙づくりから縫製まで、時間をかけて向き合っています。

手に取ってくださった方と、その人形にとって、長く大切にしたくなる一着でありますように。

About I,V

I,V is a small atelier creating handcrafted clothing for dolls. Each piece is carefully made from pattern drafting to final sewing, using natural fabrics, lace, ribbons, and softly muted colors.

Sizes

We create clothing for several doll sizes, including 1/3, 1/4, and approximately 27–30 cm dolls. Please check each collection item for details.

Sales

Items are available through selected doll events and our online shop. New sales information is announced on Instagram and X.

International Shipping

International shipping may be available by individual arrangement. Please contact us through the form above before purchasing.
